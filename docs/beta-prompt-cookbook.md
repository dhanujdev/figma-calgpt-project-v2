# Beta Prompt Cookbook

Use these prompts in `chatgpt.com` to verify the agent-only write flow and reduce cold-start confusion.

## Dashboard

- `Show my dashboard.`
- `Show my progress.`
- `Show my settings.`

## Meal Logging

- `Log breakfast: Greek yogurt, berries, and granola for 350 calories.`
- `Log lunch: chicken bowl, about 620 calories, 42g protein, 48g carbs, 17g fat.`
- `Delete my last logged yogurt meal.`

## Weight Logging

- `Log my weight as 75.8 kg.`
- `Log my weight as 167.1 lb today.`

## Coaching

- `Run my daily check-in.`
- `Run my weekly review.`
- `What should I eat next to hit my protein goal?`

## Goal And Preference Changes

- `Update my calorie goal to 2200 and protein goal to 170g.`
- `Set my goal weight to 72 kg by June 1.`
- `Use pounds for weight and turn reminder notifications off.`
